# ScribeFlow: Your Multi-Agent Letter Writing Assistant

## Design Goal
The goal of ScribeFlow is to help you write high-quality, professional letters by using a team of specialized Artificial Intelligence (AI) "agents." Instead of just one AI trying to do everything, ScribeFlow breaks the task down into specific roles—like a Project Manager, a Researcher, and an Editor—to ensure your letter is well-researched, clearly written, and ethically sound.

## How It Works
ScribeFlow achieves its goal through a structured "pipeline" or workflow:
1. **The Briefing**: You talk to the **Project Manager**. They ask you questions to understand exactly what kind of letter you need.
2. **Research**: Once the plan is clear, the **Researcher** looks for relevant information to make the letter more accurate.
3. **Writing**: The **Writer** takes the plan and the research to create a first draft.
4. **Quality Control**: The **Editor** checks the draft for tone and clarity, while the **Ethics Reviewer** ensures the letter is fair and safe.
5. **Final Touches**: If everything looks good, the **Graphic Designer** formats the letter so it looks professional.
6. **Delivery**: The Project Manager gives you the final result.

## How to Use
1. **Start a Conversation**: Type a brief description of the letter you need (e.g., "I need a formal letter to my landlord about a leaky faucet").
2. **Fill Out the Form**: The Project Manager will ask you some clarifying questions. You can fill these out in the interactive form provided.
3. **Watch the Team Work**: You'll see the "Project Pipeline" on the left update as the agents research, write, and review your letter.
4. **Review the Final Result**: Once the designer is done, the Project Manager will present you with the final, formatted letter.
5. **Adjust Settings**: If you want to change how an agent behaves, click the **Settings** icon (the gear) in the top right to edit their instructions or AI model.

## Glossary of Terms
*   **AI Agent**: A specialized version of an Artificial Intelligence that has a specific "job" or "personality" (like a Researcher or an Editor).
*   **Project Brief**: A summary of all the important details about your letter (who it's for, why you're writing it, etc.).
*   **Workflow / Pipeline**: The step-by-step process the AI agents follow to finish your letter.
*   **Markdown**: A simple way to format text (like making things **bold** or creating lists) that the AI uses to communicate.
*   **Prompt**: The instructions given to an AI agent to tell it what to do.
*   **Temperature**: A setting that controls how "creative" or "focused" an AI agent is. Low temperature means it's very consistent; high temperature means it's more imaginative.
